Dutch oven Pot Roast

Homepage

This pot roast recipe is a fabulous one pot comfort meal. The beef, carrots, and potatoes all perfectly cooked together.
This recpice is packed full of flavor and cooked all in one pot for easy and convienent cleanup.
With a recipe this good and simple you will make this part of your personal repertoire!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of boneless beef chuck roast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Kosher salt or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 large yellow onions, peeled and sliced
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3 cups beef broth, more if needed
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 springs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 pound chopped carrots
  • 1 1/2 pounds russet potatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
  2. Season the meat with the Kosher salt and ground pepper on all sides
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large 7 quart Dutch oven over medium high heat. Sear on
    each side until golden brown crust has developed, about 5-6 minutes per side. Remove
    the roast and set aside. Make sure to leave the brown bits on the bottom of the pot.
  4. Reduce to medium heat. Add the onions and saute until they start to brown,
    about 3 minutes, scraping the bottom and the sides of the pot to deglaze the
    brown bits with the moisture from the onions. Add minced garlic and cook until
    fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Add the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, fresh thyme sprigs,
    fresh rosemary sprigs, and bay leaves. Stir until combined and bring to a low boil.
  6. Return the seared meat back to the pot, the beef should be half covered by
    the liquid, if not, add more beef broth as needed. cover and bake for 1.5 hours.
  7. Remove from oven and add the chopped carrots and potatoes, check and adjust the
    seasoning at this time. Return to oven and cook for 2 hours more or until the meat
    is fork tender and falls apart, and the vegetables are tender.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
